

********************* How do Length Matching in Kicad *********************************
In Freerouting
Select Help | Help Contents in the menu bar. to open the the online help.
In the left window of the online help select Net Classes | Assign Net Class.
Then search in the right window the items min length and max length.
Length matching is only supported in interactive routing, not in autorouting.
Eventually you can correct the critical nets interactively after autorouting
